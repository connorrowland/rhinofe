Frequently asked questions

How can I trust that Rhino will reimburse me?

Rhino New York LLC is a licensed insurance producer in New York state. When a tenant becomes a Rhino member, the landlord is covered for the rental unit automatically.

What major insurance company backs Rhino?

Rhino is partnered with Canopius US Insurance, a subsidiary of Sompo Canopius, which writes more than $1.3 billion in premium annually and provides insurance services worldwide.

Are landlords covered for lost rent?

Rhino reimburses up to 2x the monthly rent should a tenant fail to pay. Just like damage claims, landlords submit reports of non-payment online. It’s quick and easy.

What if a renter stops paying Rhino?

You’re covered. Even if a tenant fails to pay our monthly fee, Rhino will reimburse landlords for up to 2x the monthly rent when the tenant is at fault.

Can existing tenants use Rhino?

Absolutely. Rhino is not just for new applicants. In fact, offering Rhino to current tenants can drive higher lease renewal rates and help long-term build trust.

What if a tenant renews their lease?

If the tenant sticks with Rhino, there’s no change. If they decide to stop using Rhino, they’ll pay a security deposit to the landlord as they normally would.
